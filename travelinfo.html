How to Reach

  • By Air:

    --Flying to Kashmir (Srinagar Airport - SXR) from major Indian cities takes about 1.5 to 2 hours in the air, but total travel time depends on connections, with flights from Delhi being around 1 hour 15 mins.

  • By Train:

    --The Kashmir Himalayas from Mumbai by train, you'll travel to Jammu Tawi (JAT) and then take a bus or cab to Srinagar, with the train journey alone taking around 30 to 38 hours.

  • By Road:

    --Driving from Mumbai to Kashmir (Srinagar) by road covers roughly 2,100-2,200 km and takes about 4-6 days.

Other options

  • Shared taxis (Sumos/Gypsies)
  • Private cabs
  • Government/private buses (JKSRTC)
