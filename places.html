Popular Attractions

1.Famous Lake

Dal Lake, Srinagar's iconic urban lake, is known as the "Jewel in the Crown of Kashmir"

2.City Palace

Pari Mahal, or the "Palace of Fairies," is a stunning seven-terraced Mughal garden and historic monument located atop the Zabarwan mountain range in Srinagar, Jammu and Kashmir

3.Ancient Temple

The Shankaracharya Temple, also known as the Jyeshteshwara Temple, is an ancient Hindu shrine dedicated to Lord Shiva, perched atop the Shankaracharya Hill (Gopadri Hill) in the Zabarwan Range of Srinagar, Kashmir.

4.Local Market

The Gulmarg Main Market is the primary commercial and social hub of the town, offering a variety of local products, essential services, and dining options.

5.Festival Ground

The Kashmir Valley Festival Ground is a vibrant venue that hosts the annual Kashmir Valley Festival, celebrating the region's rich culture, arts, and traditions through various performances, exhibitions, and activities.

6.Food Street

The Srinagar Food Street is a bustling culinary hotspot in the heart of Srinagar, offering a diverse array of traditional Kashmiri dishes and street food that reflects the rich flavors and cultural heritage of the region.
